Examples of Major Courses Offered in Popular Public Universities

  • Computer Science: Explore the world of programming, algorithms, and software development.
  • Engineering: Learn about various engineering disciplines such as mechanical, electrical, and civil engineering.
  • Business Administration: Develop skills in management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship.
  • Nursing: Prepare for a rewarding career in healthcare with courses in anatomy, physiology, and patient care.
  • Political Science: Study government systems, international relations, and public policy.
  • Psychology: Understand human behavior, cognition, and mental processes.
  • Biology: Explore the diversity of life through courses in genetics, ecology, and cellular biology.
  • Education: Learn about teaching methodologies, curriculum design, and educational psychology.
  • Journalism: Develop journalistic skills including writing, reporting, and media analysis.
